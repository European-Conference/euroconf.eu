Octavia Marginean-Tahiroglu is the General Manager of The St. Regis New York, considered one of the world's finest hotels. She is an accomplished leader with over 20 years of experience in the hospitality industry, having previously held leadership roles with The Tribeca Grand Hotel and The Soho Grand Hotel.
